Happy Hunting Grounds

Think you know your Premier League away goals? Test your esoteric football knowledge in this fiendish quiz, invented by Adam Hurrey (@FootballCliches)

How does it work?

Each game consists of 5 questions. Before each question, you must choose a difficulty level - 1 being the easiest, 10 the hardest, which will determine how many points you score for a correct answer. The question will then ask you to name a player that has scored at a certain ground, and the season they scored the goal.
